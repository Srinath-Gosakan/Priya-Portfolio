About Me

I'm a 48-year-old homemaker with two kids, passionate about designing and stitching dresses. What started as a hobby has grown into a business, taking orders from friends and relatives. I specialize in transforming traditional dresses into modern chudis, catering to a range of clients from young girls to brides. Despite offering modest pricing, I prioritize quality and customer satisfaction.
